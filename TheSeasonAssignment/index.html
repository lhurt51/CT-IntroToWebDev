The Seasons

The seasons our important to our world... So how would you describe them?

Winter

The Sound of Silence

A blanket of white!
Frosty tears of winter fall-
The sound of silence.

Elaine George

Spring

Perfect Perfume

exquisite white blossom
bathing in the sun
scents of spring cross my path

Brian Strand

Summer

All the children

A thousand small kids
Floating on the summer wind
Dandelion seeds

Anne Lise Andresen

Autum

September

As Autumn ignites,
The leaves burn with ember glow.
